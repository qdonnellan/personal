Software Development


lessonwell

www.lessonwell.com

My first startup-venture, lessonwell is a lesson/course platform concieved from two big ideas: lesson making should be simple; lessons should be accessible and look great on any device. I built lessonwell in Python in Google App Engine and hung it on Twitter Bootstrap. Lessonwell uses a mashup of Markdown, Latex, and my own shorthand to give teachers a powerfully simple text-based way of writing lessons. I manage my customers with stripe and do version control with FogCreek's Kiln.
